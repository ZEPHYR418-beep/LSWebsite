The Puzzle




What am I searching for?
So the voice inside my head asks
Everytime we share a moment

Whether it’s watching warm hues
Of the sunset or
At the tiny cubicle in the library

The pit of my stomach starts churning
Once my eyes meet his and
Then I don’t know the next move

Because those sleepless nights we have,
With each word spoken
Tells another tale every time

But once my hands wrap around his,
It’s like I found the missing piece
To my puzzle
